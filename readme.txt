Display your Twitter followers or people you are following in your sidebar.

== Description ==

With this widget you can display a grid of people who are following you on Twitter, or people you're following.

== Installation ==

1. Upload directory contents to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Check out your sidebar widgets configuration
